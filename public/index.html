01. Do I need to pay for counselling?

No. Our counselling — predictor report, college shortlisting, document help and admission process guidance — is 100% free for students & parents. We are compensated by partner colleges only after successful admission.

02. Which exams qualify for these top 11 colleges?

KCET, COMEDK UGET, JEE Main, PESSAT (for PES) and CUET (for Christ University). Management quota seats are also available across most private colleges as per their respective policies.

03. Are hostel and scholarship help included?

Yes. Once your seat is confirmed, your dedicated counsellor helps with hostel allocation guidance, government scholarship application support and education loan introductions through our network of partner banks.

04. How do you protect my personal data?

All form submissions are transmitted via 256-bit SSL encryption. Your data is used solely for counselling purposes and is never sold to third parties. You may request deletion of your data anytime by emailing getintocollege2026@gmail.com. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
